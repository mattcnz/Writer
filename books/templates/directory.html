Chasing Your Tale is a tool for writers who can start, but need a bit more motivation to finish.

If you don't write consistently, all your progress is deleted.

Access your books in progress from the taskbar, or create a new one

How It Works

It's easy! To create a book, select New Book from the books menu in the top right, or click the plus.
Decide how many words you want to write, and how many you want to write per time period. For example, 2000 words total, 500 every time period, where a time period is 24 hours.
Hit create. You need to reach your word goal in the time allotted, or your book will be deleted. When you finish, you'll be free to export your work.
